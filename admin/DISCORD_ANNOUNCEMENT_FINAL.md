**⭐ FEATURED: COUNCIL ROTATION MANAGEMENT**

**What It Does:**
Regenerates the alliance voting schedule for future weeks while preserving all past history. The algorithm ensures fair distribution of rotating council seats (ranks 6-15) with no back-to-back rotations.

**How to Access:**
1. Visit https://www.lastwar1586.online
2. Log in with your President credentials
3. Navigate to **Alliances → 🗳️ Council Rotation**
4. View status dashboard (current week, next rotation date, rotating pool)
5. Click "🔄 Regenerate Future Weeks" when needed
6. Confirm and view fairness distribution results

**When to Regenerate:**
✅ Alliance rankings change significantly
✅ New alliances enter top 15
✅ Alliances leave top 15
✅ Manual fairness adjustment needed

**What's Protected:**
• Past and current weeks are NEVER modified
• Only future weeks (next 52 weeks) are recalculated
• All actions are logged for transparency

**Algorithm Features:**
✅ Fair distribution - all rotating alliances get equal representation
✅ No back-to-back rotations (minimum 2-week gap)
✅ Graduated penalties for recent rotations
✅ Cycle tracking for long-term fairness

**❓ FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS**

**Q: How do I get President access?**
A: Contact a Server Admin. This role is reserved for top alliance leaders and trusted server representatives.

**Q: What's the difference between President and R5?**
A: Presidents can regenerate the council rotation schedule. Everything else is the same.

**Q: Is R5 better than R4 on the website?**
A: No, they have identical permissions. Both can manage all alliance communications equally.

**Q: Can Presidents delete other people's messages?**
A: No, only Admins can delete other users' messages.

**Q: Can R4/R5 see other alliances' Discord channels?**
A: No, you can only access your own alliance's configured channels.

**Q: When should I regenerate the council rotation?**
A: When alliance rankings change significantly or when new alliances enter/leave the top 15.

**Q: Will regenerating the rotation change past weeks?**
A: No, past and current weeks are always preserved. Only future weeks are recalculated.
